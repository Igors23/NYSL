Mission

To Support young athletes living in Chicago's northside neighbourhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer, with opportunities to learn to practice skills related to the game of soccer, specially those skils or around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspires to develop strong, well-rounded and minfull athletes throug the building of character, self-discipline and, leadership.

General information

The Northside Youth Soccer League was established in 1996 to providing athletes residing in Chicago's northside neighbourhoods an environment in which to learn and play socer. to be a member of NYSL, you must be between ages 4-12 and reside in Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small fulltime staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
